MasseyHacks VI

April 25th - 26th, 2020

Canada's oldest high school hackathon

MasseyHacks is a high school hackathon where 150+ students of all skill levels gather to learn, create, and experience something amazing. Over this split 24-hour event, you will find seasoned mentors to learn from, lively activities to take part in (cup stacking and hackenger hunts are a must), and a plethora of workshops to discover! Complete with three main learning streams based on your hacking experience, MasseyHacks is a place to explore the world of computer science and technology.

How much does MasseyHacks cost?

Admission to MasseyHacks VI is $25 per person. If you require a subsidy, please contact us at hello@masseyhacks.ca

What do I need to bring?

Paperwork such as your waiver and ID are necessary for entry. Bring your own laptop and charger; power strips are also recommended. As for other necessities like food and fun, rest assured that we’ve got you covered.

Who can participate?

Any student in Grades 7-12 can participate. If you've graduated from high school, you can still apply to be a mentor.

Do I need to know how to program?

Not at all — in fact, we encourage newcomers to come and participate in MasseyHacks. We’ll teach you the fundamentals of programming, tech essentials, and loads more!

How will the teams work?

How will the teams work? You can go solo and create something by yourself, but we encourage you to form a team beforehand — everything's better with friends. Teams can have up to 4 members. Don't worry if you don't have a team at the event, as we will have a team formation event for hackers to find potential team members.
