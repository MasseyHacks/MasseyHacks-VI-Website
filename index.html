MasseyHacks VI

March 28th - 29th, 2020

Canada's oldest high school hackathon

MasseyHacks is a high school hackathon where 250+ students of all skill levels — from those who haven't written "Hello World!" to seasoned hackers with SSH enabled power strips — come together to learn and create something amazing for 24 hours straight. Here you'll find free-to-use hardware, experienced mentors, activities like cupstacking and hackenger hunts, and lots of workshops! MasseyHacks is a place to immerse yourself in the massive world of computer science and engineering, create anything you want and let your imagination run free.

How much does MasseyHacks cost?

Admission to MasseyHacks VI is $25 per person. If you require a subsidy, please contact us at hello@masseyhacks.ca

What do I need to bring?

Paperwork such as your waiver and ID are necessary for entry. Bring your own laptop and charger; power strips are also recommended. As for other necessities like food, freedom and fun, rest assured that we’ve got you covered.

Do I have to stay for the entire event?

We suggest that you stay for the entire event, but if you can't stay the night, you can leave during the night and come back. If are under 18, you can get a parent to pick you up before midnight and drop you back at the event after 5 am. If you are 18, you do not need a parent.
